# Launch Copy Templates

Replace placeholders with concrete details. Keep channel norms intact.

## One-Sentence Story

```text
[Product] helps [specific user] get [specific outcome] without [painful old way].
```

Variants:

- `For [specific user], [Product] turns [old workflow] into [new result].`
- `[Product] is a [category] for [specific user] who need [outcome] but do not want [old pain].`
- `I built [Product] because [specific moment of pain]. It helps [user] [result].`

## Product Hunt First Comment

```text
Hey Product Hunt,

I'm [name], the maker of [product].

[Product] helps [specific user] [specific outcome] without [painful old way].

I built it because [short founder story or observed pain]. The current version can:

- [Capability 1 tied to outcome]
- [Capability 2 tied to outcome]
- [Capability 3 tied to outcome]

It is best for [ideal user/use case]. It is probably not right for [honest limitation].

For launch, I'm especially looking for feedback on [specific question]. If you try it, I would love to hear where the flow is confusing, what you expected it to do, and what would make it useful enough to keep using.

Thanks for taking a look.
```

## Show HN Post

Title:

```text
Show HN: [Product] - [neutral factual tagline]
```

Post:

```text
Hi HN, I'm [name]. I built [product], a [plain category] that helps [specific user] [outcome].

The problem: [describe the concrete pain without sales language].

I ran into this while [personal backstory]. Existing options were [tradeoff], so I tried [your approach].

How it works: [technical or product details HN readers can inspect].

What's different: [specific difference, not superlative].

You can try it here: [link]. [Mention no-signup/demo/free access if true.]

Pricing/access: [be transparent].

I'd love feedback on [specific technical/product question], and I'm happy to answer questions about the implementation, tradeoffs, or roadmap.
```

## Reddit Or Community Post

```text
Hi all, I'm [name], the person who built this. I checked the rules and I think this is relevant because [why it fits the community].

The problem I kept seeing was [pain]. [Product] tries to solve it by [short mechanism].

Here is the demo/link: [link]

What might be useful even if you do not use it: [lesson, benchmark, template, code, write-up, or comparison].

I'm looking for feedback on [specific question]. Happy to answer anything about how it works or why I made the tradeoffs I did.
```

## LinkedIn Launch Post

```text
I launched [product] today.

It helps [specific user] [specific outcome] without [painful old way].

The reason I built it: [professional pain, customer/user observation, or founder story].

What it does today:

- [Outcome 1]
- [Outcome 2]
- [Outcome 3]

What I learned while building it: [practical insight].

I'm looking for feedback from [specific role/persona], especially on [specific question].

[Link]
```

## X/Twitter Launch Sequence

```text
Post 1:
I launched [product]: [one-sentence story].

Built for [specific user] who are tired of [old way].

[demo/link]

Post 2:
The old way: [pain].

The new flow: [result].

[screenshot/GIF]

Post 3:
The part I'm most interested in feedback on: [specific question].

If you are [specific user], I would love for you to try it and tell me where it breaks your expectations.
```

## Direct Outreach DM

```text
Hey [name], I noticed [specific reason they are relevant].

I just launched [product], which helps [specific user] [outcome]. I thought of you because [context].

No pressure, but if this is close to a problem you have seen, I would value your blunt feedback on [specific question].

Link: [link]
```

## Newsletter Or Podcast Pitch

```text
Subject: Built [product] for [specific audience/problem]

Hi [name],

I read/listened to your piece on [specific topic]. I thought this might be relevant to your audience because [connection].

I just launched [product], which helps [specific user] [outcome] without [old way].

The interesting angle is [lesson, technical approach, market shift, unusual result, or story]. It is not just a product announcement: [why their audience would learn something].

Demo/link: [link]

If useful, I can send a short founder note, screenshots, or answer questions.
```
