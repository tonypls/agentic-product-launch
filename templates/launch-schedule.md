Rules of thumb:

- Never host two **Live** threads at the same moment — stagger them.
- Lead with the primary discovery channel, then release secondary posts in waves.
- Hold one short, honest "launch day so far" post for late in the day, once there are real numbers.

## Pre-Launch Scheduling Checklist

- [ ] Live posts confirmed for surfaces that need real-time replies (Show HN, Reddit, Product Hunt).
- [ ] Broadcast posts (X sequence, LinkedIn) queued in each platform's **native** scheduler.
- [ ] Every scheduled post has its final copy from `templates/channel-copy.md` and its asset attached.
- [ ] Links in scheduled posts resolve (GitHub release published first if referenced).
- [ ] Community rules re-read in-app for each community post, immediately before posting.
- [ ] Builder's launch-day calendar blocked to host the live threads.
