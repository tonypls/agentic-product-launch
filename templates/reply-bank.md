# Reply Bank

Use these as starting points. Answer the actual comment first, then adapt the template.

## Pricing

```text
Current pricing is [price/model]. I chose that because [reason]. If that feels off for your use case, I would love to understand what you expected.
```

## Competitor

```text
[Competitor] is a good option for [use case]. I am focused on [difference]. The tradeoff is [honest tradeoff].
```

## Missing Feature

```text
That makes sense. I left it out because [reason], but I am tracking demand for it. What would you need it to do for your workflow?
```

## Bug

```text
Thanks for catching this. I can reproduce/I'm investigating. Expected behavior is [expected]. I will update the thread when fixed.
```

## Skeptical Comment

```text
Fair pushback. The narrow case where I think this helps is [specific case]. Outside that, [limitation] is real.
```

## Privacy Or Security

```text
Good question. The current behavior is [plain explanation]. The limitation is [limitation]. If you need [higher bar], I would rather be clear that [current status] than overpromise.
```

## Roadmap

```text
I am considering [feature/area], but I am trying to keep the next version focused on [near-term goal]. What would make this a must-have for your workflow?
```
